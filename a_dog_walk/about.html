A Dog Walk

An experiment into narrative storytelling. Some panels in this comic have been drawn, and others are blank.

A dog goes for a walk. Some things happen. The question is: what happens?

Artists are invited to contribute a panel to the story, changing the narrative, and opening it up to redirection. All artists are credited.

Rules

  1. Black line art
  2. 1 additional colour of your choice
  3. 600x600px panel
  4. Use the template provided below
